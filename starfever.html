Starfever

Personal photography

During the last year I often took my old film cameras and friends on a small roadtrip. Whereever we arrived we spend the night under the nightsky - taking pictures of the moving firmament.

Most of these pictures were taken with a Zenza Bronica ETRSi and EXA A. Film stock with a speed of 400ISO and 20-120min exposure time.

Photography has been one of my favourite hobbies for years and the skills helped me a lot with the creation of digital content!
