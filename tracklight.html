Tracklight 75

Tracklight 75 lamps are hand manufactured in Germany and provide high build quality and one of the best lighting capabilities in the market.

The product photos are shot in the same rooms, where these lamps are manufactured. Still we wanted to create a professional look for these product pictures, often referred to as environmental photography.

The Tracklight lamps are shown in the foreground, yet the background is supposed to deliver further information about the product and how it's made.

The same principle applies to the additional extras around the lamp. The battery case can power up to eight lamps and is shown in action!
