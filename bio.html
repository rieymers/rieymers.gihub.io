Hallo/ Hello/ Hej

I'm Fabian, a visual designer from Germany. I exchanged the rose coloured glasses for a VR headset and have been working across all realities ever since.
Currently trying to mix realities to make data accessible in exciting new ways!

About me

I was born in the Bauhaus City Dessau and have always been interested in finding solutions and tinkering around with technology. Through the time I switched from Lego to different ways of creating models and transferring ideas!

At the moment I am studying Industrial Design at the HS Magdeburg Stendal and constantly thinking about how we can make better products. Better for us, better for the environment and with fewer used resources.

We already had a lot of oppurtunity to realize amazing real life projects, but I also can't wait to see what other possibilities are out there waiting for all of us.

When I am not designing, you'll find me in the nature, sailing or exploring my surroundings through my camera. And from time to time you will also see me contributing to the open source projects that already helped me so much on my way.
